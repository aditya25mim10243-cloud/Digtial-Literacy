# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

As a Student Digital Ambassador, I recommend following these 8 actionable steps to protect your digital identity.

## General Online Safety
1. **Enable MFA:** Use Multi-Factor Authentication on all email and social media accounts.
2. **Update Software:** Keep your operating system and apps updated to patch security vulnerabilities.
3. **Strong Passwords:** Use unique, complex passwords for different platforms; avoid using your name or birthdate.
4. **Public Wi-Fi Caution:** Avoid accessing banking or sensitive accounts while connected to unencrypted public Wi-Fi.
5. **Check URLs:** Always verify that a website address starts with "https://" and look for the padlock icon before entering data.
6. **Think Before You Click:** Do not click on links or download attachments from unknown or suspicious email senders.

## UPI & Financial Safety (Mandatory)
7. **PIN is for Sending Only:** Remember that you NEVER need to enter your UPI PIN to *receive* money.If someone asks for your PIN to "credit" your account, it is a scam.
8. **Verify Payee Details:** Double-check the name and UPI ID of the recipient on the confirmation screen before hitting 'Pay'.

## Reporting Cybercrime
If you are a victim of any online fraud, report it immediately:
**National Cyber Crime Portal:** [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in) 
  **Helpline Number:** 1930 (Available 24/7)
