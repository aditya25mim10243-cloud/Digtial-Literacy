# Social Media Etiquette & Responsibility Checklist

As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have developed this guide to help students maintain a professional and safe online presence.

## ✅ The 5 Do's
1.  **Privacy Audit:** Regularly check your account privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts and tagged photos.
2.  **Verify Before Sharing:** Fact-check news or academic information before reposting to prevent the spread of misinformation.
3.  **Professional Networking:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with alumni and industry professionals in your field of study.
4.  **Constructive Engagement:** Use social media to support campus events, share helpful resources, and celebrate peer achievements.
5.  **Think Before You Post:** Remember that your digital footprint can be viewed by future employers and internship coordinators.

## ❌ The 5 Don'ts
1.  **Oversharing:** Never post sensitive personal data like your home address, phone number, or current precise location.
2.  **Online Conflict:** Avoid engaging in aggressive arguments or "flame wars" that can damage your digital reputation.
3.  **Plagiarism:** Do not post or claim credit for code, designs, or articles created by others without proper attribution.
4.  **Unprofessional Tone:** Avoid using offensive language, excessive slang, or derogatory remarks in public or semi-private groups.
5.  **Ignoring Security:** Never click on suspicious links or "free gift" tags, as these are common phishing and malware traps.
